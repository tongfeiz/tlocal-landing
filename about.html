About tlocal

tlocal is a collaborative workspace for teams who localize on-screen text in video and long-form content. It brings detection, review, routing to language teams, and in-context mockups into one place so you spend less time on spreadsheets and handoffs.

We’re a team of builders across tech, design, and storytelling. With backgrounds in the arts, we’re building tools that accelerate workflows—without replacing human creativity.
